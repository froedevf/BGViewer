Nightscout Viewer - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Nightscout Viewer is a Roku channel that displays continuous glucose monitor (CGM) data from Nightscout and Dexcom Share. This policy explains what data the app handles and how it is used.

Data We Collect

Nightscout Viewer does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers operated by the developer. The app operates entirely on your Roku device.

Data Stored on Your Device

The following information is stored locally in the Roku device registry (on-device only):

  • Account display names
  • Nightscout server URLs
  • Dexcom Share usernames and passwords
  • Dexcom region settings

This data never leaves your device except to communicate directly with your configured Nightscout server or Dexcom Share servers.

Third-Party Services

The app communicates with:

  • Your Nightscout server - a server you control, to fetch CGM readings
  • Dexcom Share API (share2.dexcom.com or regional equivalents) - to authenticate and fetch CGM readings. Your Dexcom credentials are sent directly to Dexcom servers and are subject to Dexcom's privacy policy.

Health Data

CGM glucose readings are displayed on screen but are not stored persistently beyond the current session, and are never transmitted to the developer.

Medical Disclaimer

Nightscout Viewer is not a medical device and is not intended for use in medical diagnosis or treatment. Do not use this application to make medical decisions. Always verify glucose readings with an approved medical device.

Children

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
